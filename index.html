INTRODUCTION

We are an in the Transport industry for the last decade. We started our journey in 2012 with the Name ATHARVA TRAVELS.

We have started to explore the opportunities to serve our services to MNC companies. we were able to deliver as per Expectations to a couple of companies in Mumbai & Pune during the last few years. Our Aim is to deliver the best services during monsoon-like situations, also at the time of big festivals and BCM situations.

Our fleet consist of Tempo Travelers 17+ Seater, Cars & SUV (50+ no’s). Currently serving in 2 locations Mumbai and Pune. To Name, a Few MNC companies for which support is provided by our team are Deutsche Bank, Barclays Bank, Citibank, Accenture, Wipro, and others.

To provide an end-to-end solution for employee transportation through the combination of Automated and Manual processes. It carries the advantage of very less human intervention in day-to-day activities and results in accurate data and transparent operations.
