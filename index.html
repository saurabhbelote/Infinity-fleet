INTRODUCTION

We are an into the Transport industry for the last decade. We have started our journey in 2012 with the Name of ATHARVA TRAVELS.

We have started to explore the opportunities to serve our services for the MNC companies. we were able to deliver as per Expectation to a couple of companies in Mumbai & Pune during last few years. Our Aim to deliver best services during monsoon like situations, also at time of big festivals and BCM situations.

In our fleet consist of Tempo Travelers 17+ Seater, Cars & SUV (50+ no’s). Currently serving in 2 locations Mumbai and Pune. To Name a Few MNC companies for which support is provided by our team are Deutsche Bank, Barclays Bank, Citibank, Accenture, Wipro and others.

To provide an end-to-end solution for employee transportation through the combination of Automated and Manual process. It carries an advantage of very less human intervention in day to day activities and results into accurate data and transparent operations.
